The Estate

A Catalan Treasure,
Exclusively Yours

Ses Garites is one of the most extraordinary historic estates on the Costa Brava — a 16th-century Catalan manor set in the medieval village of Pals, moments from the Mediterranean. With original stone vaulted ceilings, exposed wooden beams, and terracotta floors that have stood for over 500 years, the estate offers an unrivalled backdrop for weddings, private retreats, and exclusive gatherings.

The entire estate is available only as a whole — never shared, never divided. Your event, your home, your story.
